CoreBR: A Collection of Brazilian Papers in Major CS Conferences (alpha version)

CoreBR collects papers published by Brazilian authors in major Computer Science conferences. The papers are automatically retrieved from DBLP. Currently, CoreBR covers Software Engineering and Programming Languages papers; but we plan to cover other areas and also to provide statistics about departments and researchers.
