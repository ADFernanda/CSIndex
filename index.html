CoreBR: Exploring Brazilian Scientific Production in Computer Science

CoreBR aims to provide meaningful and transparent data about Brazilian scientific production in Computer Science. Currently, the project focus on full research papers published in selected conferences. The papers are retrieved from DBLP. See our FAQ for details or open an issue.
