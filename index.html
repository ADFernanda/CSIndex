CoreBR: Crawling Brazilian Papers in Core CS Conferences (alpha version)

CoreBR collects (from DBLP) papers published by Brazilian authors in major Computer Science conferences. It is inspired in the CSRankings site, but considering more conferences. In this version, CoreBR covers only Software Engineering papers. But we are actively working to cover other areas.
